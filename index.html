Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Hannah, Patisserie Hana is a French-inspired bakery dedicated to crafting handcrafted pastries, artisan breads, and beautifully made desserts using premium ingredients and traditional baking techniques.

Guided by a passion for freshness and quality, our mission is to create French-inspired baked goods that bring joy to every customer through exceptional taste, careful craftsmanship, and warm service.
